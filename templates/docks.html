Global Pacific Properties Basement loading dock

$20 fee

GPP basement loading dock can accommodate large moving trucks and trailers. The garage entrance is 14ft tall located on 15th Street, after California before Stout. Access to Neusteters is through the inner garage door with the freight elevator 50 yards from dock. Pass can be purchased from GPP office at 1520 Stout St.

Alley-way Loading Dock

Free to use but often blocked

Alley way is free to use but during the week it is often blocked by delivery trucks. There are also barriers that have been added at 15th St. Barriers are bendable and can be driven over. Access is through our trash/recycling dock with freight elevator 10ft from dock. Its beneficial that the moving truck has a side door.
