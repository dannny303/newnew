Rules and Regulations regarding Home Occupations

The following rules and regulations have been adopted by the Neusteters Building Condominium Association, INC("Association") pursuant to Colorado statutes, for Home Occupations.

  1. The condominium Declaration for Neusteters Building, DEfinitions (Section1,y) states " ' Residential Unit' shall mean those UNits established herein, as delineated on the Condominium Map, which shall be used and occupied soley for residential purposees."
