The Residences at Neusteters

The Neusteter building has a long history, 107 years to be exact. Originally the building was a three story department store bought from Mc.Phillson Co. in 1911. In 1920's the building was expanded from 3000 sqft to 9000 sqft.

Once known as "The fashon empire of the rocky mountains!" Sometime in the 1950's the Neusteter Co. expanded again to its current size.

The Neusteter Co. closed its doors in the 1980's and began its second life as an 80 unit apartment building, one of the first few residential buildings downtown. The aprtments sold off into condos in the early 1990's. National Register of Historic Places 1987.
